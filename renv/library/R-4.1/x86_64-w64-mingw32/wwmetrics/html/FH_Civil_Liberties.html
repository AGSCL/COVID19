FH_Civil_Liberties {wwmetrics}R Documentation

Civil liberties (Freedom House)

Description

Data on civil liberties for 196 countries from 1972 to 2016.

Usage

data("FH_Civil_Liberties")

Format

A tibble with 8021 observations and 4 variables:

  • ID. A factor indicating the country (ISO3 code) and the year.

  • Country. Factor indicating the country (ISO3 code).

  • year. Integer indicating the year.

  • FH_Civil_Liberties. Factor "measured on a one-to-seven scale, with one representing the highest degree of Freedom and seven the lowest." (source: https://freedomhouse.org/sites/default/files/Country and Territory Ratings and Statuses FIW1973-2018.xlsx)

Source

https://freedomhouse.org/report-types/freedom-world

References
