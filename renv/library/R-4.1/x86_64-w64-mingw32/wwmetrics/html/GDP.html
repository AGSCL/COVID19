GDP (Gross Domestic Product)

Description

Data on the GDP of 256 countries from 1960 to 2016.

Usage

data("GDP")

Format

A tibble with 11542 observations and 4 variables:

  • ID. A factor indicating the country pair (ISO3 code) and the year.

  • Country. Factor indicating the country (ISO3 code).

  • year. Integer indicating the year.

  • GDP. Number indicating the amount of GDP in USD.

Source

https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/NY.GDP.MKTP.CD

References

http://www.worldbank.org/
