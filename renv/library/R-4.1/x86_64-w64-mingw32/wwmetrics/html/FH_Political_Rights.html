FH_Political_Rights {wwmetrics}R Documentation

Political rights (Freedom House)

Description

Data on political rights for 195 countries from 1972 to 2016.

Usage

data("FH_Political_Rights")

Format

A tibble with 8001 observations and 4 variables:

  • ID. A factor indicating the country (ISO3 code) and the year.

  • Country. Factor indicating the country (ISO3 code).

  • year. Integer indicating the year.

  • FH_Political_Rights. Factor "measured on a one-to-seven scale, with one representing the highest degree of Freedom and seven the lowest." (source: https://freedomhouse.org/sites/default/files/Country and Territory Ratings and Statuses FIW1973-2018.xlsx)

Source

https://freedomhouse.org/report-types/freedom-world

References

https://freedomhouse.org/
