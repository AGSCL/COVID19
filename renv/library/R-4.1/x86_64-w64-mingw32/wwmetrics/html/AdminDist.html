AdminDist {wwmetrics}R Documentation

AdminDist

Description

Data on Administrative distances based on the Worldwide Governance Indicators by the World Bank.

Usage

data("AdminDist")

Format

A tibble with 786 173 observations and 12 variables:

  • ID. Factor indicating the countries forming each country pair (ISO3 code) and the year.

  • year. Integer indicating the year.

  • CountryPair. Factor indicating the country pair (ISO3 code).

  • origin. Factor indicating the first country (ISO3 code) of the country pair.

  • destination. Factor indicating the second country (ISO3 code) of the country pair.

  • AdminDist. Numeric. Administrative distance as the difference in the average of the scores obtained on the six following Worldwide Governance Indicators.

  • diffr_in_Control_of_corruption. Numeric. Difference in the score the World Bank granted the origin country and the destination country for each year on the indicator Control of Corruption.

  • diffr_in_Government_effectiveness. Numeric. Difference in the score the World Bank granted the origin country and the destination country for each year on the indicator Government Effectiveness.

  • diffr_in_Political_stability_and_absence_of_violence_terrorism. Numeric. Difference in the score the World Bank granted the origin country and the destination country for each year on the indicator Political Stability and Absence of Violence and Terrorism.

  • diffr_in_Regulatory_quality. Numeric. Difference in the score the World Bank granted the origin country and the destination country for each year on the indicator Regulatory Quality.

  • diffr_in_Rule_of_law. Numeric. Difference in the score the World Bank granted the origin country and the destination country for each year on the indicator Rule of Law.

  • diffr_in_Voice_and_accountability. Numeric. Difference in the score the World Bank granted the origin country and the destination country for each year on the indicator Voice and Accountability.

Source

https://datacatalog.worldbank.org/dataset/worldwide-governance-indicators
